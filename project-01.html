About Us

Established in the ancient city of Nasik in 1987, Rushiraj Group came together with a vision to re-define the residential and commercial real estate business and has never looked back ever since.

Rushiraj Group is proud developers of 25+ lacs sq ft of prime real estate, over the last three we have earned an unmatched reputation for global standards of construction quality and state of the art lifestyle projects in the most premium real-estate locations across the city.

The place we belong to becomes the root for us to pursue all our hopes and aspirations. It's from where we connect with the world and ourselves, where we shape our identities, dare to dream and embark on your journey towards a brighter tomorrow.
